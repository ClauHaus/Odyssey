How to play Odyssey

In order to clear the levels in Odyssey you must flip the "cards" by clicking them and match the pairs, taking in consideration the "remaining moves" (how many mistakes you can make) which are going to get lower as long as you mismatch the pairs of cards, and the "remaining time". When you match correctly a pair of cards the "moves to win" is going to go lower, when that indicator reaches "0" that means you just cleared the level and you can move into the next one.
